About Us

Govinda's Prasadam

Govinda’s Prasadam is an initiative by ISKCON Hadapsar dedicated to serving sanctified, satvik, and pure vegetarian meals prepared with deep devotion and hygiene.

Our kitchen follows strict spiritual standards, offering food that nourishes not just the body but also the soul.

Terms of Use

Terms & Conditions

1. Satvik Policy: All preparations are strictly vegetarian (No meat, fish, eggs) and free from onion and garlic.

2. Cancellations: Once an order enters the 'Cooking' stage, it cannot be cancelled as food preparation has begun.

3. Delivery: Delivery is handled by temple volunteers. Timings are estimates.
